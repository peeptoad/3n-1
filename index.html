3n + 1

3n+1 also known as The Collatz conjecture is one of the most infamous unsolved problems in mathematics.The problem is simple choose any positive number from 1 to ∞. You will then apply two rules to the number if its odd multyply it by 3 and add 1 if the number is even divide it by 2 and keep repeating these steps. The conjecture is that by applying these rules to any positive interger it will result in a 4,2,1 loop. While no one has proved the conjecture as of 2020 it has proved true for every number less than 295,147,905,179,352,825,856.

Example: 5 odd × 3 + 1 = 16 even ÷ 2 = 8 even ÷ 2 = 4 even ÷ 2 = 2 even ÷ 2 = 1 odd × 3 = 3 + 1 = 4 even ÷ 2 = 2 even ÷ 2 = 1
